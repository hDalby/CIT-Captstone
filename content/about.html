A Love for Horses and Coffee

Our passion is to grow your love for coffee, friendship, and family by bringing a delicious coffee experience to you. This 1970's horse trailer has been converted into a mobile coffee house that delivers the finest espresso sourced from one of Oklahoma's finest coffee roasters.

We specialize in catering private parties and weddings but can also be found at special events all across this great state.

The owners are a husband and wife team that met while working at a coffee shop. Their love for each other and coffee has grown over the years and finally blossomed into their own coffee shop.

When they are not manning the mobile coffee shop they can be found chasing their kids and animals around on their family farm in Wellston, OK.
