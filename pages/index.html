WELCOME TO THE PALOMINIO




At Palomino Coffee Co. we are about giving ya'll the finest quality coffee and the purest ingredients to create a wholesome mix of excitement and energy.





























A coffee shop with an in-house roaster, our roasters have a wealth of experience in helping customers understand, taste and evaluate coffees so we know our beans best. We always meet our commitment to our customers, making sure that our coffee is as fresh as possible..



We roast at a fairly regular time of approximately mid-September to mid-November, with the bulk of our work focussed around this time. We roast in small batches and are committed to sourcing only the highest quality beans. As we are a small operation we are able to focus our efforts on quality, the very best tasting coffee and, most importantly, on producing the very best quality coffee. We roast in small batches with the intention of getting the highest quality coffee in as much volume possible. We roast at a nice, even temperature, and take great care to source only the finest coffee available from the best regions, rather than roasting coffee at high temperatures.







The name "Palomino" is derived from the first horse purchased at the Dalby Farm. We started The Palomino Coffee Company in 2021 to bring our passion and expertise in coffee to the public. Our mission is to be the first coffee roaster in the Oklahoma, providing our customers with the highest quality coffee available. We believe that we have created a brand that represents the best in the Oklahoma coffee industry. Our motto is Coffee and Cowboy Up. Our roasting roasts are always done by our own talented roasters. We are always looking for new and exciting ways to improve the quality of our coffee.
